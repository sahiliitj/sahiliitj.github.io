About Sahil

Hi! I'm Sahil Sharma — a Data & Computational Science enthusiast pursuing my M.Sc-M.Tech dual degree at IIT Jodhpur. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous mathematics and modern AI, with a particular focus on Generative AI and Quantum Machine Learning.

I currently work as a Gen AI Developer at Axtria, where I build intelligent systems for pharma commercial analytics. I was recognised as Top Performer in the Campus Training Program 2025.

My research journey has taken me through Google Research & IIT Roorkee (image regeneration models), IRDE, DRDO (image denoising for defence applications), and contributing to a book chapter published by CRC Press, Taylor & Francis, USA.

I believe the most powerful AI systems are those built on a deep understanding of mathematics — probability, optimisation, stochastic processes — not just empirical tricks. That belief drives everything I build and research.
